What do yoga classes bring me?

Difficulty level: low to medium - depending on the trainer & type of yoga Calorie consumption: between 180 and 300 calories per hour Effect: More mobility, inner balance, positive energy and a better body feeling

What is mindfulness?

Mindfulness is the practice of gently focusing your awareness on the present moment over and over again. It often involves focusing on sensations to root yourself in your body in the here and now. It can be practiced during formal meditation or during everyday activities, like cooking, cleaning, or walking.

What is meditation?

The word originally comes from the Latin "meditatio" and means "to think" or "to be deep in thought". In fact, meditation is a conscious (partially spiritual) practice of concentration . The rumor that the mind is completely switched off when meditating is only partially true.
